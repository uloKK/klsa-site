KINGSTON LEFTIST STUDENTS' ALLIANCE

WHAT WE ARE

  • We are a collective of high school students operating from KCVI, Kingston, Ontario.
  • We are a left-wing activist group.
  • We are anti-capitalist, anti-racist and anti-fascist.
  • We are for the liberation of working people from oppressive power structures.
  • We are for direct action, good praxis and resisting any and all forms of injustice.

WHO WE ARE

We are a diverse coalition of leftists that have put our differences aside in favor of unity, solidarity, comradery and keeping alive the struggle for the greater good.

An intersectional alliance, there are many different ideologies to be found among our members, ranging from anarchism to democratic socialism to anything else left-wing.

What we have in common is our left wing belief system, our sense of justice and our desire to bring about radical social change.
